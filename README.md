# 🧠 WiseCruit — AI Recruitment Intelligence Engine

**Hire Smarter, Hunt Less. Hit Gold.** 🥇

WiseCruit is an AI-driven role fit analysis and automated deep shortlisting engine built for the **Redrob AI Hackathon — India Runs**.

## 🎯 What WiseCruit Does

Unlike simple keyword matchers, WiseCruit evaluates candidates across **8 dimensions**:

1. **Skill Match** — Semantic alignment with JD, weighted by proficiency & endorsements
2. **Career Match** — Title relevance, ML experience in job descriptions
3. **Experience Fit** — Years aligned with 5-9 year target
4. **Education Fit** — Degree, institution tier, field relevance
5. **Location Fit** — Geographic match & work mode preference
6. **Company Fit** — Product vs consulting background detection
7. **Hiring Readiness** — 23 behavioral signals analysis
8. **Trust Score** — Honeypot detection & profile authenticity
